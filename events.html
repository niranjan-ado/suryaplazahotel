The Pearl Banquet Hall

The Perfect Venue for Every Occasion

Discover the premier function area in Sahibganj to celebrate your event in style. The "Pearl" at Surya Plaza Hotel is a perfect multi-functional banquet and conference hall, situated on the fifth floor.

It is a versatile venue with modern amenities and a stylish setting, spanning 1500 sq. ft. with a flexible capacity for 30 to 150 guests. Whether you are planning a wedding, reception, business conference, or birthday party, we provide the perfect backdrop and impeccable service for your elegant event.
