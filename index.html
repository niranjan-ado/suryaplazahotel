Welcome to Surya Plaza Hotel

Situated at the heart of the city, Surya Plaza Hotel is where timeless hospitality meets contemporary comfort. Designed for discerning travellers, the hotel blends elegant modernity with a warm reflection of local culture.

Run by a family of doctors, our roots in healthcare inspire an uncommon philosophy of hospitality, one defined by precision, cleanliness, and genuine care. Every detail, from the freshness of our linens to the warmth in our service, reflects a deep respect for your well-being.
