About the Author

“My passion lies at the intersection of technology and hospitality. It’s about more than just building a website; it’s about capturing the soul of a place like Surya Plaza Hotel—translating the warmth of its service and the beauty of Sahibganj into a digital experience that helps you, our guest, discover what makes this place special.”

Niranjan Ramamurthy

With a background in digital marketing and content strategy that began in the early 2000s, Niranjan brings a data-driven yet creative approach to storytelling. His work, through his agency Adostrophe, focuses on using high-fidelity photography and clear, helpful content to bridge the gap between a guest's online research and their real-world experience at our hotel.
