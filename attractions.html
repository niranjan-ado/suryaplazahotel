Local Attractions

Top Places to Visit

Moti Jharna Waterfall

A scenic and popular waterfall in the Rajmahal hills, known for its natural beauty and lush green surroundings, with water falling over two ledges of rock.

directions_car 34 min drive (14 km)

Shivgadi Dham

A revered Lord Shiva temple located inside a cave, where water from the mountain drips continuously onto the Shiva Lingam. A major site for devotees.

directions_car 1 hr 24 min drive (58.6 km)

Sahibganj Ghati

A well-known river port on the banks of the Ganga River, offering beautiful scenic views. It has historical significance as a major transportation and trade hub.

directions_car 5 min drive (1.4 km)

Historical Monuments

Akbari Masjid

One of the oldest mosques in the region, representing Mughal-era architecture from when Rajmahal was the capital of Bengal.

directions_car 1 hr 13 min drive (37.3 km)

Singhi Dalan

Dating from the mid-17th century, this "Hall of Stone" is one of the principal remaining sections of Sultan Shah Shujaâ€™s palace.

directions_car 1 hr 14 min drive (37.4 km)

Baradari

Once the residence of a confidant of Emperor Akbar, this palace with 12 doors sits on the shore of the Ganges and is preserved by the ASI.

directions_car 1 hr 3 min drive (30.9 km)

Temples & Religious Sites

Bindabasini Temple

Also known as Bindudham, this ancient temple is a Shaktipeeth dedicated to the Tridevi, with prominence regained by the work of Pahari Baba.

directions_car 1 hr 49 min drive (73.3 km)

Jama Masjid, Rajmahal

Built by Man Singh, governor-general of Akbar, in the same style as the Delhi Jama Masjid. It is located facing the Baradari palace.

directions_car 1 hr 58 min drive (65.7 km)

Rani Sati Dadi Mandir

A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Rani Sati Dadi, symbolizing feminine bravery and devotion. The temple hosts various religious events.

directions_car 5 min drive (1.6 km)

Local Landmarks

Bhagwa Kuan

A historical well built in 1815 during the British Raj. It remains a masterpiece of construction and is still functional with drinkable water.

directions_car 1 min drive (130 m)

Chowk Bazar

A vibrant and bustling marketplace in the heart of Sahibganj. It serves as a central hub for shopping, cultural events, and daily city life.

directions_car 1 min drive (290 m)

Sahibganj Science Centre

The second science center in the state, featuring interactive exhibits, 3D printing, robotics, and a unique exhibit on local Jurassic-era fossils.

directions_car 8 min drive (1.8 km)
