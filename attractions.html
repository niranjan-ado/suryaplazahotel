Top Places to Visit

Moti Jharna Waterfall

A scenic waterfall in the Rajmahal hills, known for its lush surroundings and water cascading over two ledges.

directions_car 34 min drive (14 km)

Shivgadi Dham

A revered Lord Shiva temple inside a cave, where water naturally drips onto the Shiva Lingam. A major pilgrimage site.

directions_car 1 hr 24 min drive (58.6 km)

Sahibganj Ghati

A historic port on the banks of the Ganga River, offering beautiful scenic views and a glimpse into its past as a trade hub.

directions_car 5 min drive (1.4 km)

Temples & Religious Sites

Also known as Bindudham, this ancient temple is a Shaktipeeth dedicated to the Tridevi, drawing many pilgrims.

Built by Man Singh, governor-general of Akbar. It is a grand mosque located opposite the Baradari palace.

A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Rani Sati Dadi, symbolizing feminine bravery and devotion.

Local Landmarks

Bhagwa Kuan

A historical well built in 1815 during the British Raj. It remains a masterpiece of construction and is still functional.

directions_car 1 min drive (130 m)

A vibrant and bustling marketplace in the heart of Sahibganj. It serves as a central hub for shopping and daily city life.

The second science center in the state, featuring interactive exhibits and a unique display on local Jurassic-era fossils.

Visitor Information

Sahibganj is famous for its unique blend of natural beauty and historical significance. It is known for attractions like the scenic Moti Jharna waterfall, the sacred Shivgadi Dham cave temple, and its historic port on the banks of the River Ganges. The area is also notable for its Jurassic-era fossils.

The best time to visit Sahibganj is during the winter months, from October to March. The weather during this period is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and exploring the local attractions.

A trip of 2 to 3 days is generally sufficient to explore the main attractions of Sahibganj and the surrounding Rajmahal hills at a comfortable pace. Our hotel's central location makes it an ideal base for your exploration.
