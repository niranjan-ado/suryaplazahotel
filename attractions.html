Top Places to Visit

Moti Jharna Waterfall

A stunning scenic waterfall nestled in the Rajmahal hills, renowned for its lush green surroundings and water cascading over two beautiful ledges.

Shivgadi Dham

A revered Lord Shiva temple located inside a natural cave, where water perpetually drips onto the Shiva Lingam. A major pilgrimage site.

directions_car 1 hr 24 min drive (58.6 km)

Sahibganj Ghati

A historic port on the banks of the holy Ganga River, offering breathtaking scenic views and a glimpse into its past as a vital trade hub.

Visitor Information

Sahibganj is famous for its unique blend of natural beauty and historical significance. It is known for attractions like the scenic Moti Jharna waterfall, the sacred Shivgadi Dham cave temple, and its historic port on the banks of the River Ganges. The area is also notable for its Jurassic-era fossils.

The best time to visit Sahibganj is during the winter months, from October to March. The weather during this period is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and exploring the local attractions.

A trip of 2 to 3 days is generally sufficient to explore the main attractions of Sahibganj and the surrounding Rajmahal hills at a comfortable pace. Our hotel's central location makes it an ideal base for your exploration.
