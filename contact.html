Frequently Asked Questions

Our room rates are dynamic to offer the best value, but they typically start from ₹2099 for a Club Comfort Room. For the most accurate and up-to-date pricing for your travel dates, we always recommend checking our official booking engine.

For families and larger groups, we offer two excellent options. Our Family Apartment is an expansive 800 sqft space featuring two bedrooms and a full kitchen, perfect for longer stays. The Two-Bedroom Premium Suite is another great choice, offering two bedrooms, a separate drawing room, and beautiful river views.

Yes, we are proud to host two of the city's finest dining establishments. Guests can enjoy legendary Indian and Chinese cuisine at our fine-dining restaurant, Moti Mahal, or opt for a more casual experience at the rooftop Jackson Diner, which serves pizzas, pastas, and gourmet mocktails. We also provide 24/7 in-room dining from our restaurant menus.

Our hotel is centrally located in the heart of Sahibganj, a key town on the banks of the river Ganges. We are the perfect base for travelers, situated just minutes from the main railway station, the steamer ghat (ferry terminal), and the bustling Chowk Bazar. Whether you are visiting for business or exploring local attractions, our prime location ensures you are conveniently connected to everything the city has to offer.
