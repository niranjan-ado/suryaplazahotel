Shipping Policy

While Surya Plaza Hotel is primarily a provider of hospitality services and does not operate as a retail shipping business, we offer assistance with shipping-related matters as a courtesy to our guests. This policy outlines how we handle such requests.

1. Scope of Policy

This policy applies to:

  • Handling of packages delivered to the hotel on behalf of a registered guest.
  • Assistance in shipping items left behind by guests after check-out.

2. Incoming Packages

  • Guests wishing to send packages to the hotel prior to their arrival must ensure the package is addressed to the name on the reservation with the check-in date clearly marked.
  • The hotel will securely store incoming packages for a reasonable period. We are not liable for any loss or damage to items before they are claimed by the guest.

3. Lost & Found Items

  • Items found in rooms after guest departure will be logged and stored securely for a period of 30 days.
  • If you believe you have left an item, please contact our front desk immediately with your room number and stay dates.
  • If the item is located, we can arrange for it to be shipped to you. The guest is responsible for all associated shipping and packaging costs, which must be paid in advance.

4. Shipping Charges & Liability

  • All costs associated with shipping, including packaging, postage, and any applicable insurance, are the sole responsibility of the guest.
  • We will facilitate the shipment through a reputable third-party courier. Once an item is handed over to the courier, Surya Plaza Hotel is no longer liable for any loss, damage, or delays in transit.
  • We will provide tracking information to the guest once the shipment is dispatched.

5. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this policy or to arrange for a shipment, please contact our front desk.
