Your Questions, Answered

Our fine-dining restaurant, Moti Mahal, and our rooftop restaurant, Jackson Diner, are both open for lunch and dinner. We also offer 24/7 in-room dining service for our resident guests.

Absolutely. We welcome both hotel guests and non-resident visitors to experience the culinary delights at Moti Mahal and Jackson Diner. We recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends.

We offer a diverse range of culinary experiences. Moti Mahal is renowned for its authentic North Indian, Mughlai, and Tandoori cuisine. Jackson Diner offers a more casual menu featuring gourmet pizzas, pastas, burgers, and shakes.
