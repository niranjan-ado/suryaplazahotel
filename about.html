About Surya Plaza Hotel

An Uncommon Philosophy of Hospitality

Situated at the heart of the city, Surya Plaza Hotel is where timeless hospitality meets contemporary comfort. Designed for discerning travellers, the hotel blends elegant modernity with a warm reflection of local culture.

Run by a family of doctors, our roots in healthcare inspire an uncommon philosophy of hospitality—one defined by precision, cleanliness, and genuine care. From the freshness of our linens to the warmth in our service, every detail reflects a deep respect for your well-being, ensuring an experience that is both personal and restorative.

Come, discover a place where hospitality is not just offered; it's practised with heart, discipline, and grace.
