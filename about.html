About Surya Plaza Hotel

Your Sanctuary in Sahibganj

Run by a family of doctors, Surya Plaza Hotel is inspired by a unique commitment to precision, cleanliness, and genuine care. Situated in the heart of Sahibganj, we blend sophisticated comfort with vibrant local culture, offering an ideal choice for both business and leisure travellers.

Our professional team is dedicated to delivering a warm ambiance and an array of thoughtful touches, ensuring every guest experiences the comforts of home with the sophistication of contemporary hospitality.
