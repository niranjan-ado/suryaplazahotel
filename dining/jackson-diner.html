Casual Rooftop Dining

Jackson Diner by Moti Mahal

Your Perfect Rooftop Escape

Welcome to Jackson Diner, a casual dining restaurant located on the rooftop of Surya Plaza Hotel. Here you can enjoy an array of gourmet offerings in a warm, vibrant, and comfortable ambience.

Our menu features a wide variety of mocktails, shakes, sandwiches, burgers, pizzas, and pastas. It's the perfect spot to unwind with friends, enjoy a family meal, or simply relax and take in the view.
