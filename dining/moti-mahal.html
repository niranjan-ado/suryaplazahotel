A Royal Culinary Experience

Moti Mahal Tandoori Trail

The Legacy of Authentic Indian Cuisine

Located within Surya Plaza Hotel, Moti Mahal Tandoori Trail brings an authentic taste of North Indian, Mughlai, and Tandoori cuisine to Sahibganj. Known for its rich legacy and signature flavors, this restaurant offers a dining experience like no other.

Part of the iconic Moti Mahal brand, famous for introducing Butter Chicken and Dal Makhani to the world, we continue a tradition of culinary excellence. Indulge in a rich and aromatic selection of dishes, all prepared with the freshest ingredients.

Our Signature Dishes

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Our signature dish. Tender tandoori chicken simmered in a creamy, tomato-based gravy that originated from our kitchens.

Dal Makhani

Black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked for hours with butter and cream to achieve a rich, velvety texture.

Tandoori Chicken

A classic preparation where chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a tandoor for a smoky, succulent flavor.

Mutton Rogan Josh

A flavorful curry with tender mutton pieces cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy, known for its deep red color.
