Your Home Away From Home in Sahibganj

Our Family Apartment is the ultimate choice for extended stays or families seeking the comforts of home. This expansive 800 sqft apartment includes two private bedrooms, a separate drawing room with a sofa cum bed, and the unparalleled convenience of a fully equipped kitchen, complete with an induction cooktop, microwave, and utensils. The modern, clean bathroom ensures a comfortable stay for all guests. Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the River Ganges and the surrounding hills from your two private balconies.
