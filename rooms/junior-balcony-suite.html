A Suite Experience in Space & Luxury

Boasting a generous 234 sq. ft., our Junior Suite provides an elevated experience for guests seeking more space and comfort. Enjoy a restful night’s sleep on the plush double bed and unwind in the separate living area, which features a comfortable sofa perfect for relaxation or casual conversation. The private balcony adds a touch of serenity, offering a quiet space to enjoy the views. The suite includes a modern, well-lit bathroom with premium fittings and a refreshing shower, ensuring a luxurious stay from start to finish.
