Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Surya Plaza Hotel, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be as flexible as possible while allowing us to manage our reservations effectively. Please review the following terms carefully.

Standard Cancellation Policy

  • Free Cancellation: Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to **24 hours** prior to the check-in date.
  • Late Cancellation: For cancellations made less than 24 hours before arrival, a fee equivalent to the first night's stay will be charged.
  • No-Show: In the event of a no-show (failure to arrive without prior notification), the total cost of the first night will be charged to the card on file, and any remaining nights on the reservation will be cancelled.

Advance Payment & Non-Refundable Bookings

Some promotional rates or packages require an advance payment and may be explicitly marked as “non-refundable.”

  • These bookings are not eligible for cancellation or modification. No refunds will be issued for these reservations.
  • Please review the rate details carefully before confirming a non-refundable booking.

Refund Process

  • Eligible refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.
  • Refunds for early departures are subject to the terms of the rate plan booked and are not guaranteed.

How to Cancel

  • Direct Bookings: If you booked directly via our website or by phone, please contact our reservation desk to process your cancellation.
  • Third-Party Bookings: If you booked through an online travel agency (e.g., Booking.com, MakeMyTrip), you must cancel through their platform, subject to their terms and conditions.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding our cancellation policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
